Characters

Here are a few of the friendly faces you'll see around the city of Crushington Park:

Meteor

This fearless moon rover was built to explore the outer reaches of space. He loves his mom and his hero is the world champion, Bigfoot. Meteor is equipped with lots of high tech gadgets and a camera to record his friends awesome jumps on the race track.

Catch phrase: BLAST OFF!

Look for Meteor in the episode: Race Relations

Little Tow

Little Tow, or "LT" to his friends, loves nothing more than to tow his friends out of the mud. One day he hopes to be the world champion like his dad, Bigfoot. Little Tow likes to play practical jokes and tow his friends out of the mud.

Fun fact: José is his cousin.

Look for Little Tow in the episode: A Big hook for Little Tow

Pony Tail

Sweet and spunky, Ponytail - or just "Pony" for short - is a top competitor in her own right. She's the best jumper in the group and is quick to befriend someone new.

Catch phrase: HORSE POWER!

Look for Ponytail in the episode: Monster Trucking Today

Junkboy

Junkboy is the lovable trash hauler who would rather splash in the mud and gunk than win the race. He may not be big on personal hygiene, but his off-beat sense of humor keeps the others smiling.

Catch phrase: Fan-STINKIN'-tastic!

Look for Junkboy in the episode: Bath time for Junboy

José

Brave and headstrong, José leads the pack of other monster trucks in many of their adventures. He is the fastest of the group and can even shoot water from his built-in fire hose!

Catch phrase: FIRE IT UP!

Look for José in the episode: Team Work

Sarge

The tough little military truck, Sarge would rather smash through something than jump over it. He is a loyal friend and hopes to join the Army like his father someday.

Catch phrase: WRECK AND ROLL!

Look for Sarge in the episode: Where's Wheelie?

Amby

Amby is the caring and nervous junior ambulance. He may lack in confidence but he's always there to help a friend in an emergency.

Look for Amby in the episode: Over Heated

Big Wheelie

Big Wheelie is the teacher at Crushington Park Elementary. He was also a champion off-road racer back in the day. He cares deeply about his students and wants to see the best for them.

Look for Big Wheelie in the episode: Rebel Rover

Bigfoot

Bigfoot is the world champion in racing, stunts and crushing! Along with his wife, Rosa, he has two children, Little Tow and Zooey.

Fun Fact: Even though he has been a long time rival of the Crushmeister on the race track, they're actually best friends.

Look for Bigfoot in the episode: Like Father, Like Son
